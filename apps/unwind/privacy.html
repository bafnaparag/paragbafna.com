Privacy Policy for Unwind

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your data while helping you establish healthy break habits.

Last Updated: April 15, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Unwind, a product of ParagBafna Apps. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our break reminder application for macOS.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access or use the application.

Information We Collect

Usage Data

Unwind collects the following types of information to provide and improve its functionality:

  • Activity Metrics: Information about your computer usage patterns, including active time, idle time, and break adherence. This data is used to provide you with activity statistics and improve break recommendations.
  • Break History: Records of when breaks were taken, skipped, or postponed to provide you with insights into your break habits.
  • Application Usage: Statistics about which applications you use and for how long. This information helps provide more meaningful activity reports.
  • Settings and Preferences: Your configured break schedules, theme preferences, and feature settings to maintain your personalized experience.

Data Storage

All data collected by Unwind is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your usage data on external servers unless you explicitly enable optional cloud backup features.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information Unwind collects for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the Unwind application
  • To calculate and display activity statistics and break recommendations
  • To personalize your experience with customized break schedules
  • To determine when to trigger breaks and notifications
  • To generate visualizations of your computer usage and break habits
  • To detect when you're naturally away from your computer (to avoid unnecessary breaks)
  • To implement features like natural break detection
  • To troubleshoot and address technical issues if you request support

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. Your data collected through Unwind stays on your device unless you explicitly choose to share it. Potential instances where limited data may be shared include:

  • With Your Consent: If you explicitly enable optional features that require data sharing, such as syncing your settings across devices.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, such as to comply with a subpoena or other legal process.
  • Aggregated or Anonymized Data: We may share completely anonymized, aggregated statistics about app usage to improve our products or in research about break habits. This information cannot be linked back to any individual user.

Third-Party Services and Integrations

Unwind may offer integrations with third-party services such as calendar applications, music players, or productivity tools. When you choose to enable these integrations, please be aware that third parties have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you connect with Unwind.

AppleScript and Automator Integration

Unwind allows you to trigger AppleScript and Automator workflows during breaks. These scripts run locally on your computer and may interact with other applications. We do not monitor or collect information about custom scripts you create or use, and we are not responsible for the behavior of custom scripts.

Theme Engine Security

Unwind's theme engine uses a sandboxed web view to display custom break themes. If you create or install custom themes, please be aware that they run in a restricted environment with limited access to your system. We recommend only using themes from trusted sources.

Your Rights

Since Unwind stores data locally on your device, you have full control over this data. You may:

  • Clear your activity history through the application settings
  • Export your data for your own records
  • Reset all application data to default settings
  • Uninstall the application, which will remove all associated data (unless you've made separate backups)

Children's Privacy

Our application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information as soon as possible.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
